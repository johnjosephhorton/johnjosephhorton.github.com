About

I am an economist and an Assistant Professor in the Information Systems group at New York University's Stern School of Business. Prior to joining Stern, I was the staff economist at oDesk, an online labor market. My academic research is primarily focused on issues in market design, labor economics and organizational economics, particularly in the context of online labor markets.
